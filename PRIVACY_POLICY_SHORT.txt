PRIVACY POLICY FOR CHINESE CHESS (中国象棋)

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

SUMMARY: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Chinese Chess is a completely offline game that:
✓ Does NOT require internet connection
✓ Does NOT collect personal data
✓ Does NOT track user behavior
✓ Does NOT use analytics
✓ Does NOT display ads
✓ Does NOT share data with third parties

LOCAL DATA STORAGE:
The app stores these items ONLY on your device:

- Game statistics (scores, play time)
- Audio preferences

This data never leaves your device.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY:
Safe for all ages. Does not collect information from children under 13.

PERMISSIONS:

- Audio: Play sounds and music

DATA SECURITY:
Since we don't collect data, there's no risk to your privacy.
