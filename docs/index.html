Strong AI Engine

Alpha-beta search with null move pruning, late move reductions, Zobrist hashing, killer & history heuristics. 5 difficulty levels up to Master (depth 7).

Endgame Puzzles

8 classic endgame positions: Double Cannon, Iron Door Bolt, Heaven-Earth Cannons, Horse-Rear Cannon, and more.

Hint System

Stuck on a move? Tap the hint button and the AI will suggest the best play for your position.

Classical Design

Wood-grain board with 3D convex pieces, smooth animations, haptic feedback, and warm gold selection glow.

Save & Resume

Games auto-save when you leave. Come back anytime and pick up right where you left off.

Game Replay

Step through your game move by move. Review your strategy and learn from your decisions.
